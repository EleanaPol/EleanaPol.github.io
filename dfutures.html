Planet EmTech

June 2020

Planet EmTech is a multiplayer game that was developed for the "Digital Futures 2020" workshop lead by the "Emergent Technologies and Design" program of the Architectural Association.












Project Description

Design

The workshop participants were asked to design parts of an imaginary galaxy that followed a particular spiral-like formation, by using various computational methods.

The participants were then introduced to Unity and were asked to customize the look of their planets as well as design their avatars.

At the end of the workshop, their designed planets were placed in a multiplayer game where they were able to collectively explore the emergent gallaxy, navigate the the different planets and experience their spatial qualities.

Project Info

Role

- Lead Developer.
- Unity tutorials to participants.
- Multiplayer set-up development.
- Game hosting and networking.
- Gameplay design.
